About

Fried duck eggs are similar to fried chicken eggs but tend to be richer and more flavorful due to the higher fat content and larger yolk size. Duck eggs have a creamier texture and a slightly stronger taste, which makes them popular in gourmet cooking. Here's a simple way to fry duck eggs:
Ingredients:
- 1-2 duck eggs
- 1-2 tbsp of oil or butter
- Salt and pepper (to taste)
Instructions:
1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add oil or butter.
2. Crack the duck egg into a bowl, then gently pour it into the heated
3. Cook for about 3-4 minutes, or until the whites are set and the yolk reaches your preferred doneness.
- For sunny-side up: Cook without flipping.
- For over-easy or over-medium: Flip the egg gently and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
4. Season with salt and pepper, then serve.
